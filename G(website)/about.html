Brooklyn
Brooklyn Restaurant, a fine dining restaurant, conceptualized in the latter end of 1979, is the success story and brainchild of renowned entrepreneur Abdulla Mannakuzhy. Nestled in the capital city of Gods own country, the restaurant has created a niche for itself in the world of Modern Middle-Eastern Cuisine and promises further delight with time to come.Despite its relaxed ambiance this informal restaurant is wonderfully glamorous, both by day and by night making it the perfect spot to dine with your friends and family. Since inception, there has been a real passion to serve delicious meals in which there was no compromise on freshness, quality, hygiene and healthiness.
Brooklyn Restaurants took assured steps of success by the grace of Allah with the starting of Brooklyn Ice Fruits in 2002 followed by a new outlet in Calicut in 2005. Based on the enormity of the success and support shown to us by our customers we have initiated Zam Zam Mini, our latest venture, through which we hope to bring about new standards to fine dining in Trivandrum. A part from the initaitive to take successful restaurants further ahead scaling towards common people in country, Brooklyn Dosa Hut has been endured for Low budget restaurants in 2013.
