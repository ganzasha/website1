 BROOKLYN. 		Brooklyn Restaurant, a fine dining restaurant, conceptualized in the latter end of 1979, is the success story and brainchild of renowned entrepreneur Abdulla Mannakuzhy. Nestled in the capital city of Gods own country, the restaurant has created a niche for itself in the world of Modern Middle-Eastern Cuisine and promises further delight with time to come.Despite its relaxed ambiance this informal restaurant is wonderfully glamorous, both by day and by night making it the perfect spot to dine with your friends and family. Since inception, there has been a real passion to serve delicious meals in which there was no compromise on freshness, quality, hygiene and healthiness.
